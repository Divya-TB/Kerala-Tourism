Angamoozhy coracle Ride
Jan 2018 • Friends
Boating in Coracles made of Bamboo is major attraction in Kerala eco tourism. The coracles can take a load of six persons at a time. The coracles are locally called Kotta Vanchi in Malayalam.
You will be taken on a traditional round shaped boat.The design of the boat is unique to the rivers of southern India.Its an amazing experience which shows the beauty of the wild. Amazing kotta vanchi ride through the thick wild forest.

In my opinion Its a best place to enjoy a cool morning or a chilly evening.

Coracles can load of Six persons. Boating Charges in Kerala are Rs 400 per boat for one hour boating.

I will visit konni in the first week of october....I am interested in Kotta Vanchi ride. Kindly guide me regarding the bookings and timings.
NEERAJ J
18 contributions
Kotta Ranchi ride is at a place called Adavi, which is 13 km from Konni Elephant Park. You would require private mode of transportation in order to facilitate this journey. If possible, time your ride in the Kotta Vanchi in such a way that you reach Adavi before 10 am or after 3 pm, so as to avoid the wrath of the sun. Even if you reach there at noon, the ride is through a serene river that has trees growing over the river and therefore is enjoyable at noon also. However, the former would be better. The exact timing at Adavi can be checked online, but I remember it as 8:00 am to 5:30pm.
